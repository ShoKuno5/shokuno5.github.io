# About Me

I am a PhD student at the University of Tokyo, researching 3D object generation
within a world models team. My work sits at the intersection of geometric deep learning
and generative AI.

Current research interests:
- 3D object generation and world models
- Video and visual generation

Past research:
- (Master's thesis) Generative Modeling of Graph-Structured Data via Latent Flow Matching
- (Industry Internship) Graph-Based Approaches on Anime Frame Interpolation
- (Bachelor's thesis) Time-series prediction of a dynamical system via Reservoir Computing
